Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How do I join the club?

Simply fill out the contact form above selecting "I want to join the club" as the subject, or attend any of our weekly Tuesday meetups. Membership is free and open to all students and professionals interested in technology.

❓ Do I need prior coding experience?

Not at all! We welcome members of all skill levels. We have dedicated beginner tracks, mentorship programs, and study groups to help you get started regardless of your background.

❓ How can my company sponsor an event?

We'd love to partner with you! Please reach out through the contact form with "Sponsorship inquiry" selected. We offer various sponsorship tiers with branding, recruiting, and speaking opportunities.

❓ Are events open to non-members?

Most of our workshops and speaker events are open to the public. Hackathons and exclusive networking events are typically members-only. Check the event description for details.

❓ Can I propose a project or event?

Absolutely! We encourage member-led initiatives. Use the contact form with "Collaboration proposal" or bring your idea to our weekly meetups. The leadership team reviews all proposals monthly.

❓ Is there a membership fee?

No, membership is completely free. We are funded through university support, corporate sponsorships, and community donations. All resources, workshops, and events are provided at no cost to members.
